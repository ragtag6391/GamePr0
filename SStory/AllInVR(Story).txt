All IN (Cardboard VR)

Once, the main character was a prosperous businessman who had a good family and stable life. Unfortunately, an unwise choice after another took him to the bottom, causing him to sink into a world of gambling and drinking. Hoping to win the money he lost, the protagonist borrowed money from loan sharks, hoping for one successful game to return everything. Yet, his debts only grew with time. 

One night, after what he thought was a regular visit to a bar, the hero was ambushed by four masked men and taken captive. After waking up, he realised that he was trapped in a secret underground casino. A voice said that he had to pay $100 million and the only way to do it was through gambling. 

With what little starting money he has, the player must try to earn its riches in casino games. Each game may be his final one since everything depends on his decisions. Will he manage to settle his debts and receive his freedom, or will he lose everything? 

Every bet brings you one step closer to freedom... or one step closer to your grave.
